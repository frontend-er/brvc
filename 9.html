Children

Did you know that there are over 600,000 children living in Moldova? Children are our future, and their well-being is crucial for the prosperity of our society. At our organization, we understand the importance of investing in our children's development, and we're proud to say that we've already seen some remarkable success stories in Moldova. However, there are still many challenges that children in Moldova face, such as poverty, lack of access to education and healthcare, and social exclusion. We also invite you to join us in prayer for Moldova's children, so that they may thrive and have a brighter future. Violence. 2 in 3 children in Moldova experience some form of violence before the age of 18. Prevalence of physical violence against children: 60% . Prevalence of sexual violence against children: 1 in 5 girls and 1 in 10 boys (UNICEF) Child health. Infant mortality rate: 7.3 deaths per 1,000 live births. Prevalence of stunted growth among children under 5: 17.9% (UNICEF, 2021). Prevalence of anemia among children under 5: 25.9% (WHO, 2021) Education. Net enrollment rate in primary school: 88.7%. Net enrollment rate in secondary school: 77.6%. Completion rate of primary education: 82.6%. Completion rate of lower secondary education: 65.7% (UNICEF, 2021) Child poverty. 1 in 4 children in Moldova live in poverty (UNICEF, 2021). Child poverty rate increased from 33.5% in 2012 to 40.1% in 2019 (National Bureau of Statistics, 2020)
