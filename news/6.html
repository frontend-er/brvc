New " Gain Altitude" Educational Manual, specially designed for children 5-6 years old!

BRVC Kids

What does it include? The book consists of 40 lessons. Each lesson includes a narrative, craft, song, presentation or video. The lessons cover the five areas of a child's personality and include topics on physical, social, emotional, intellectual and spiritual development. The textbook can be used by educators, teachers as alternative education. The textbook is designed for group work, but can easily be adapted to different circumstances and opportunities. What else is there to know? The project includes six manuals for different ages from 3 to 14 years old. Both print and online versions of the textbooks for preschoolers ages 3-6 are currently available for purchase. The textbook for ages 3-4 is also available in Romanian.

The new season is unique as it is included in the large educational project "Building Resilience in Vulnerable Communities 22-25" in which the model of integrated early childhood development will be thoroughly tested and studied on a number of levels and measurements. This project is carried out in close cooperation with partners from the UK and Bulgaria.
