BRVC Teens

Congratulations to our first-graders who have successfully completed an intensive course designed specifically for young learners! We take great pride in graduating two groups, each comprising ten students representing both Russian and Romanian communities. The lessons throughout the program were based on the BRVC curriculum, renowned for its effectiveness in practice. The students thoroughly enjoyed the lessons, fostering their growth and providing them with extraordinary skills.
