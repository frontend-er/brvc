Preparing official launch of BRVC

BRVC

October 13 at 15.00-17.00 in the building of the MEDIACOR media center on the street. Alexei Mateevici 60, Chisinau) will host the official opening ceremony of the educational project "Building Resilience in Vulnerable Communities 2022-2025" in partnership with the Foundation for Social Change and Inclusion UK. This event will outline the main goals and aspects of the project and outline the main contextual framework. Representatives of the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Moldova represented by Mr. Minister Anatolie Topal, the office of the President represented by Maia Sandu, as well as the British Ambassador to Moldova Stephen Mark Fisher were invited to the opening ceremony. Representatives of public organizations from Moldova, Bulgaria, Serbia, Romania and Croatia will also take part in the event. All interested persons and organizations are welcomed to take part in this event.
